# Frever Fitness v11.5 — Routine Workout Engine

## Added
- Routine-aware workout controls for Standard, Superset, Circuit and Growing Ladder routines.
- Next exercise group, Next superset, Complete circuit round and Complete ladder step actions.
- Circuit and Growing Ladder time caps with visible countdowns.
- Growing Ladder progression: 10; then 10 + 9; then 10 + 9 + 8, and so on.
- Back navigation for standard, superset and ladder routines.
- Routine type labels in the routine list.

## Changed
- Circuit builder asks for a time cap rather than a fixed repeat count.
- Ladder builder includes a time cap and clearer example text.
- Active routine structure persists with the existing active-workout draft.
